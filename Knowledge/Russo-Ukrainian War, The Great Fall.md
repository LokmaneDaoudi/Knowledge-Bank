**MOC**: [[Worldbuilding MOC]]

In 2022, the [[Russo-Ukrainian war]] escalated to unprecedented levels.
- Escalations caused governments to prepare for [[WWI]] due to the severity of the conflict and the involvement [[NATO]] countries against the [[Russian]] government.
- The tension led to worst global market crash in history, which was called [[The Blackfall]]
#### Consequences of [[The Blackfall]]

***Economically***
- Wipe out of vast amounts of capital which led to current banking system failure worldwide.
- Disruption is supply chain, and increase in protectionism which collapsed the global trade and the world faced decline in industrial production, and high rates of unemployment
- Key sectors like manufacturing, and agriculture faced crises which led to food shortage and scarcity of basic good

***Politically***
- Middle class are met with a decrease in savings and livelihood, pushing societies to extreme ideologies/authoritarian regimes
- A shift in government due to economic consequences caused by [[The Blackfall]]
- A dramatic change in geopolitics
- Withered [[international institutions]], and the rise of fragmented multipolar world

***Tech***
- Technology is monopolized by giant tech companies/ and weaponized against consumers
- Information warfare and propaganda exploiting polarizing populous
